# Terms of Service

This repository provides a development microservice to sync personal fitness data
(steps, calories, sleep, HRV, weight) from Fitbit and other providers into a
Firebase-backed backend for personal analytics and testing.

Use at your own risk. This project is intended for personal or development use
only. Do not use this service for production or to collect data from other
people without implementing proper security, consent, and data retention
policies.
